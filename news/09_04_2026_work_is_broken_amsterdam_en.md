# Work Is Broken. Unlocking Human Agency — Amsterdam

Yesterday, as an official DevRev partner we joined DevRev's 'Work Is Broken. Unlocking Human Agency' event in Amsterdam.

What struck us most was the depth and honesty of the conversation. Organizations are no longer asking whether AI will change how they work — they know it will. The question now is whether they have the right strategy, architecture, and mindset to lead that change with confidence.

The afternoon brought together an incredible lineup of speakers: Patrick van de Werken, Dheeraj Pandey, Eric Adams, Martijn Bosschaart, Richard Lansdorp, Damien Katris, Tjerk van der Lande, Rowena Hehanussa, Sunil Dutt and Mark Raben.

A few insights that stayed with us:

🏗️ **The how matters — architecture matters.** The gap between a compelling demo and real deployment almost always comes down to how well your data, systems, and workflows are designed to support AI at scale. This isn't a technical footnote — it's the strategic foundation.

🚀 **Getting into the 5%.** A lot of companies stagnate after their first POCs. The companies that succeed define the problem first, orchestrate systems, partner smart, and focus on real business transformation. They align business ownership, prioritize integration over experimentation, and measure outcomes from day one — that's how they break through.

🧠 **Intelligence is the moat. Models are the commodity.** Every organization has access to the same tools — GPT, Claude, Gemini. The real differentiator is context: your customer data, your product knowledge, your institutional memory. The model matters less than what you feed it.

🔄 **AI is a journey, not a switch you flip.** The organizations seeing real impact are treating it as a continuous capability — not a one-off project. It requires a shift in mindset.
