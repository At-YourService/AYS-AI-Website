# Business Development Manager – AI Services

Help build the future of AI. From strategy to implementation. We are a fast-growing AI scale-up with one mission: making organisations genuinely smarter with AI. No buzzwords — just working solutions that create real impact.

That's why we're looking for a Business Development Manager who not only closes deals but also truly understands what we build. Someone who thrives on winning new clients, but gets just as excited about a good model, a smart architecture or a successful implementation.

## What will you do?

### Create new business

- You open doors at mid-sized and large organisations.
- You build your own pipeline and close deals independently.
- You spot opportunities in the market and translate them into concrete AI propositions.
- You sit at the table with management, innovation and data teams.

### Make a difference through substance

- You contribute to AI strategy, use cases and technical solutions.
- You support presales, workshops and proof-of-concepts.
- Where needed, you can roll up your sleeves on a prototype or technical validation.
- You bridge the gap between business and tech.

### From deal to lasting relationship

- You stay involved after the contract is signed.
- You safeguard value creation and customer satisfaction.
- You identify and realise upsell and growth opportunities.

## Who are you?

- 5+ years of experience in business development, tech consultancy or AI/IT sales.
- You genuinely understand AI (machine learning, data pipelines, LLMs, automation).
- You can talk about models, but also about ROI.
- You are entrepreneurial, self-driven and comfortable with targets.
- You get energy from building — both client relationships and the organisation.
- You communicate clearly and persuasively (NL & EN).

Bonus points if you:

- Have programmed yourself or have data science experience.
- Have previously worked in a startup or scale-up.
- Have a strong network in a specific sector.

## What do we offer?

- A challenging role in a fast-growing team.
- The opportunity to work with the latest technologies.
- A competitive salary package and flexible working conditions.
