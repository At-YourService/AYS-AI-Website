# Computer Conversations Rotterdam

We're bringing DevRev's Computer Conversations to Rotterdam. Join us for a hands-on workshop where you'll meet and work with Computer, your AI teammate from DevRev. Following the momentum of Leadership Circle Week in Amsterdam, this is where inspiration turns into execution.

On June 11, we gather at the iconic Van Nelle Fabriek — a UNESCO World Heritage Site designed around light, openness and flow. Because design is everything — and that same principle now applies to how we design AI inside our organisations.

Who should attend

Forward-leaning leaders and builders across Product, Engineering, IT, Support, and Revenue Operations — those responsible for systems, workflows, and customer support & experience, and ready to move from theory to real application.

What you'll do

This is a hands-on, collaborative session where you will:

- **Meet Computer** – Get introduced to your AI teammate through a live, hands-on demo

- **Build an agentic workflow** – Create and personalise a workflow tailored to your environment

- **Use Computer as a teammate** – Apply it across real use cases and workflows

- **Design an advanced agent** – Build and deploy a customer-facing agent and experience how simple it actually is

Date: June 11
Time: 12:00 PM – 6:00 PM, including lunch & drinks

Location: Van Nelle Factory, Van Nelleweg, 3044 BC Rotterdam, Netherlands
