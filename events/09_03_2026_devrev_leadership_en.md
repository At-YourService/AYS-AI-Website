# DevRev Leadership Circle Amsterdam 2026

Early access to DevRev Leadership Circle Amsterdam 2026!

Join us to see AI agents in action and experience how machines and people collaborate seamlessly to give us something priceless: our evenings and weekends back. Let's fix work — because we all know it's broken.

- Navigating information asymmetry with real-time, data-driven intelligence

We look forward to welcoming you this April and diving deep into how AI is reshaping leadership, work and the opportunities ahead.
